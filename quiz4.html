Compared to SIGINT during the Vietnam War, what is one major way modern signals intelligence has changed?
In Vietnam, SIGINT focused heavily on intercepting HF/VHF radio and Morse code, with a lot of manual traffic analysis and direction-finding flights. Today, agencies deal with oceans of digital data—phone calls, internet traffic, satellite links, and more—and lean on automation and machine learning to flag patterns that human analysts could never find fast enough.
